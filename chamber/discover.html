“Borongan is a city located on the eastern coast of the island of Samar in the Philippines. Known for its lush forests, majestic rivers, and beautiful beaches, Borongan is rich in cultural heritage and natural beauty. It is the capital of Eastern Samar and serves as a gateway to exploring the hidden gems of the region.”
